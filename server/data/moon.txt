Moon represents emotions, inner world, habits, and instincts.
Moon sign reveals how you process feelings and what makes you feel secure.
Moon is the unconscious mind and your emotional responses.
Moon in Aries often indicates impulsive emotions and quick reactions.
Moon in Aries people feel things intensely but move on quickly.
Moon in Taurus often indicates steady emotions and need for stability.
Moon in Taurus people find comfort in routine and physical pleasures.
Moon in Gemini often indicates curious and talkative emotional nature.
Moon in Gemini people process feelings through discussion and logic.
Moon in Cancer often indicates emotional sensitivity and nurturing tendencies.
Moon in Cancer people are deeply attached to home and family.
Moon in Leo often indicates dramatic and proud emotional expression.
Moon in Leo people need appreciation and attention to feel secure.
Moon in Virgo often indicates analytical and practical emotional processing.
Moon in Virgo people worry and analyze their feelings.
Moon in Libra often indicates need for harmony in relationships.
Moon in Libra people avoid conflict and seek peace.
Moon in Scorpio often indicates intense and secretive emotional nature.
Moon in Scorpio people feel everything deeply.
Moon in Sagittarius often indicates optimistic and restless emotional nature.
Moon in Sagittarius people need freedom to feel happy.
Moon in Capricorn often indicates reserved and controlled emotional expression.
Moon in Capricorn people take relationships seriously.
Moon in Aquarius often indicates detached and unconventional emotional nature.
Moon in Aquarius people process feelings intellectually.
Moon in Pisces often indicates highly sensitive and psychic emotional nature.
Moon in Pisces people absorb emotions from others.
