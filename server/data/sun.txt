Sun is the core of who you are and what drives you.
Sun sign determines your basic personality and character traits.

Sun in Taurus people value security and material comfort.

Sun in Gemini people are quick-witted and love to learn.
Sun in Cancer often indicates emotional sensitivity and nurturing tendencies.
Sun in Cancer people are deeply connected to home and family.
Sun in Leo often indicates creativity, drama, and natural leadership.
Sun in Leo people thrive on attention and self-expression.
Sun in Virgo often indicates analytical thinking and attention to detail.
Sun in Virgo people are perfectionists who love to serve.
Sun in Libra often indicates diplomacy, harmony, and partnership.
Sun in Libra people seek balance and fairness in all things.
Sun in Scorpio often indicates intensity, passion, and transformation.
Sun in Scorpio people are deeply emotional and resourceful.
Sun in Sagittarius often indicates optimism, adventure, and philosophy.
Sun in Sagittarius people love freedom and exploration.
Sun in Capricorn often indicates ambition, discipline, and responsibility.
Sun in Capricorn people are hardworking and goal-oriented.
